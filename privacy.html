Privacy Policy

Gravity Flip · Last updated: 17 July 2026

This privacy policy explains how Gravity Flip (“the app”), developed by NeacsuClaudiu, handles user information. By installing or using the app you agree to this policy.

1. What data we collect

The app itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as name, email, phone, or exact location) to our servers. We do not run any server that receives your data.

All game progress (skins unlocked, coins, best scores, missions, achievements, settings) is stored only on your device using the operating system’s local storage. It is not synced to the cloud and it is deleted when you uninstall the app.

2. Third-party services (advertising)

The app displays advertising provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect information required to serve ads, including but not limited to:

  • your device’s Advertising ID (a resettable identifier you control from your device settings);
  • coarse device information (model, operating system, language, general region derived from your IP address);
  • ad interaction events (an ad was requested, shown, dismissed, or rewarded).

Google AdMob’s handling of this information is governed by Google’s privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

On first launch in the European Union, the app shows a consent form (Google’s User Messaging Platform) that lets you accept or refuse personalized advertising. You can change your choice at any time by reinstalling the app or by resetting your Advertising ID from your device settings.

3. In-app purchases

The app offers an optional one-time purchase called Remove Ads that permanently disables advertising. The purchase is processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not see or store your payment details. The only information stored on your device is a flag noting that the purchase was made, so ads stay disabled after a reinstall.

4. Data we do NOT collect

  • No account creation, sign-in or login is required.
  • No email address, phone number, or contacts.
  • No microphone, camera, precise location, or media library access.
  • No analytics or crash-reporting SDK is embedded beyond what AdMob requires for ad delivery.

5. Children

Gravity Flip is a casual arcade game suitable for all ages. It is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them. Because advertising is served by third parties, we recommend parental supervision for younger players. Parents may contact us to request that any device-level data be removed from AdMob (see contact below).

6. Your rights

If you reside in the European Union, the United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with equivalent data protection laws (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you have the right to:

  • refuse personalized advertising through the consent form shown on first launch;
  • reset or delete your Advertising ID from your device settings at any time;
  • delete all app data by uninstalling the app;
  • contact Google to exercise data-subject rights against AdMob directly.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always available at neacsuclaudiu.github.io/gravity-flip/privacy.html. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
